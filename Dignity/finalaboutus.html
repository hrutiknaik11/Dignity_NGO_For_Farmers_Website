Our Inspiration

DIGNITYwhich is Inspired from the Slogan of JAI JAWAN ,JAI KISAN. A Country Needs a Solider Who Secures nation at Border also a Farmer who Secures by producing food.Where as Farmer is Not less than a Solider.But that Farmer is being neglected in our society .So ,We as a Agriculture Students feel Responsible to help farmers with the help of Youth and Expertise of agriculture .

DIGNITY is Team of young and Enthusiastic Agricultural Students Who are Pursuing Their Post Graduation .We want to Create a Responsive Team for Building a Society who Cares For our Farmers. We determined to Help Farmers in Possible ways for Making their life better and becoming their voice.
